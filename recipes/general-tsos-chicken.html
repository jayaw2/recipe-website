General Tso's Chicken

Description

General Tso's Chicken is a very popular option that you'll see at almost all of your campus's local Chinese restaurants. It is a great combination of sweet and tangy, and will add lots of flavor to your boring ol' chicken breast. Have it alongside white rice or chow mein, as well as steamed vegetables of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Tsang's General Tso's Chicken Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Recipe

  1. Slice the chicken breasts into small cublets.
  2. Take out a large pan and heat on medium-high with olive oil.
  3. Put the sliced chicken breasts in the pan.
  4. Season the chicken with salt and pepper while cooking.
  5. After 2-3 minutes, apply a quarter of the Tsang's General Tso's Sauce botttle to the chicken, because you'll want some flavor.
  6. Cook until chicken loses its pink, and serve over white rice pr noodles and steamed vegetables.
